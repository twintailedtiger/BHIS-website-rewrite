Why Us?

Why Us?

As a world-class school with a holistic approach to education, Beijing Horizon International School has strived for excellence since it's inception. We aim to nurture well-rounded students from kindergarden to Grade 12. Students at BHIS learn through challenging yet joyful experiences, and are destined for success upon graduation.

About BHIS

Founded in 2013 by Scarlett W. Hanner, BHIS started off as a humble one-building school in Shunyi. Mrs. Hanner believed that a focus on holistic education and well-rounded students are vital in creating successful global citizens.
